### Your First 5 Minutes
**Step 1: Sign Up**  
1. Open the app  
2. Enter email or phone  
3. Verify with OTP  
4. Enable biometrics (Face ID/fingerprint)

**Step 2: Create Your Wallet**  
<hint style="warning">**Important:** Your 12-word seed phrase is your only backup. Write it down securely‚ÄîSwipeMe can't recover it for you.</hint>

- Self-custodial wallet generated on-device  
- AES-256-GCM encryption  
- Biometric protection for everyday use  

**Step 3: Fund Your Wallet**  
Add stablecoins via:  
- Transfer from MetaMask/Trust Wallet  
- Exchange withdrawal  
- On-ramp partners (coming soon)

**Step 4: Send Your First Swipe**  
1. Start a chat  
2. Tap $ icon  
3. Choose amount + stablecoin  
4. Confirm with biometrics  
5. Done in ~0.5 seconds  

<hint style="success">**Boom!** You just sent money faster than a text.</hint>

## Core Features

### Encrypted Messaging
Powered by XMTP‚Äîtrue end-to-end encryption.

**What This Means**  
- Messages encrypted on your device  
- Only you and the recipient can read them  
- Servers never see content  

**Supported Types**  
- Text, voice messages, images  
- Disappearing messages  
- Group chats (up to 100)  

**Lifecycle**  
1. You type ‚Üí encrypted locally  
2. Sent via XMTP network  
3. Recipient decrypts on their device  

Read receipts: ‚úì sent ‚Ä¢ ‚úì‚úì delivered ‚Ä¢ ‚úì‚úì‚úì read

### Instant Payments
Send stablecoins directly in chat‚Äîsettled on Tempo blockchain.

**Specs**  
- Finality: ~0.5 seconds (deterministic)  
- Throughput: 100k+ TPS  
- Fees: Ultra-low, stablecoin-native  

**Supported Coins**  
- pathUSD (default)  
- AlphaUSD, BetaUSD, ThetaUSD  

**How It Works**  
1. Tap $ in chat  
2. Enter amount + optional memo  
3. Biometric confirm  
4. Instant on-chain settlement  
5. Receipt appears in chat  

<hint style="success">**Real finance:** Money received at 10:00:00 can be re-spent at 10:00:01.</hint>

**Fees**  
- Send: Just network gas (~$0.001)  
- Creator tips: 5% platform (95% to creator)  
- Withdraw: Gas only  

### Wallet Management
Your money, your control.

**Key Details**  
- Self-custodial ‚Äì you hold the keys  
- Encrypted with AES-256-GCM  
- Biometric access for daily use  
- 12-word seed for recovery  

**Backup Tips**  
<hint style="warning">**Never** screenshot your seed phrase. **Never** share it. Write it down and store safely.</hint>

**Actions**  
- Send/receive any Tempo stablecoin  
- View full transaction history  
- Export for taxes
