FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SwipeMe.

1 What is SwipeMe?
SwipeMe is a super app that combines end-to-end encrypted messaging with blockchain-based P2P payments, a social Moments feed, creator monetization, and a mini-apps marketplace. It's like WeChat, but decentralized and built on blockchain technology.
2 Is my money safe?
Yes. SwipeMe uses a self-custodial wallet, meaning you control your private keys. Your wallet is secured with AES-256-GCM encryption and protected by biometric authentication. We never have access to your funds.
3 What stablecoins are supported?
SwipeMe supports multiple stablecoins on the Tempo testnet including pathUSD, AlphaUSD, BetaUSD, and ThetaUSD. You can send and receive any of these tokens within the app.
4 Are my messages private?
Absolutely. All messages are encrypted using the XMTP protocol with end-to-end encryption. Only you and your recipient can read your messages - not even SwipeMe can access them.
5 How do creators get rewarded?
Creators get rewarded through direct tips from fans on their Moments posts. Earnings accumulate in real-time and can be withdrawn weekly on Mondays UTC. It's a simple and transparent way to monetize your content.
6 Is SwipeMe available now?
SwipeMe is currently in early access on the Tempo testnet. Join our waitlist to be among the first to experience the app when we launch publicly.
