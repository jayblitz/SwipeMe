Features

Everything You Need in One App

SwipeMe combines the best of messaging and payments into a seamless experience.

End-to-End Encrypted

Your messages are secured with XMTP protocol encryption. Only you and your recipient can read them - not even us.

Instant P2P Payments

Send money to friends directly from your chat. No delays, no middlemen - just instant transfers on the Tempo blockchain.

Multi-Stablecoin Support

Pay with pathUSD, AlphaUSD, BetaUSD, or ThetaUSD. Choose the stablecoin that works best for you.

Self-Custodial Wallet

You control your keys, you control your funds. Your wallet is secured with biometric authentication and encrypted storage.

Voice Messages

Record and send voice messages with WhatsApp-style waveform visualization and playback speed controls.

Custom Chat Themes

Personalize each conversation with custom backgrounds. Choose from preset colors or create your own unique look.
