# Create an Account

### Sign up account <a href="#sign-up-account" id="sign-up-account"></a>

1. Click on the Sign Up to create a SwipeMe Account

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/2026-01-14 13.41.12.jpg" alt="" width="284"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



* Follow the prompt to create a password, confirm your email, choose a username and done.
* Once you login to your account, you can choose to enable PassKey for easy login.
* If you want to enable passkey, "Go to Profile" -> "Click on Passkey" -> enable "Use Face ID or Touch ID to log in".<br>
