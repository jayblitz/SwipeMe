# Introduction

## Welcome to SwipeMe

SwipeMe is the decentralized super app that combines end-to-end encrypted messaging with lightning-fast stablecoin payments on Tempo. Chat securely, send money in seconds, tip creators instantly, all in one place. No banks. No hassle. Your keys, your money.

### The Problem We Solve

Chatting is easy, but paying is still clunky. Remittance huddles, high fees, No monetization in certain Countries, no privacy. SwipeMe fixes that by making finance feel as natural as a conversation.
