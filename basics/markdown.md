# Generate a wallet

To generate a wallet, click on Create New Wallet.\
\
A 12 word passphrase will be generated. Keep it safe. If you lose it, you lose your assets.\
&#xNAN;_**Note: Do not share this with anyone.**_&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/2026-01-14 14.20.06.jpg" alt="" width="284"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

_**Check the box to indicate you have saved your recovery phrase after you've saved it and done. Wallet created.**_ <br>
