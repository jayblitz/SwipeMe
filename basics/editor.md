# Import an existing wallet

To import your wallet to SwipeMe, there are two ways.

### 1. Import Passphrase

* Copy your 12 secret words and paste them accurately and in the right manner on SwipeMe.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/2026-01-14 14.14.44.jpg" alt="" width="284"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### 2. Import via Private Key

* Your Private Key is similar to your wallet address but is longer than the address. Its begins with "0x"
* Copy it and paste in SwipeMe to import your wallet.
