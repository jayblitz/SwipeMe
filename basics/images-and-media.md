# Fund your wallet

Fund Your Wallet Add stablecoins via:

* Claim Faucet from Tempo
* Exchange withdrawal
* On-ramp partners (coming soon)

> Since we are in testnet, we will cover how to claim tokens from Tempo Testnet Faucet

#### How to Claim test tokens

1. Go to Wallet section
2. Click on the copy icon to copy your wallet address
3. Click on Get Free Tempo, enter your wallet address on tempo faucet page and click on **Add funds.**

#### Send Your First Swipe

Now that you've received the test tokens, send your first Swipe!

1. Start a chat
2. Tap $ icon
3. Choose amount + stablecoin
4. Confirm with biometrics
5. Done in \~0.5 seconds
